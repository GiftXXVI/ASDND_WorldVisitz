What is Agile?

A new approach to developing software. Also applicable to product development in general. Agile increases the focus on:

  1. Engagement with business users.
  2. Delivery of business value.

The Agile Manifesto

After years of developing software, the the developers who created the Agile methodology discovered that:

  • Individuals and interactions generate more value than processes and tools.
  • Working software is more valuable than comprehensive documentation.
  • Customer collaboration delivers more value than contract negotiation.
  • Responding to change creates more value than following a plan.
