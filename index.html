How WorldVisitz Can Benefit From Agile?

What is an Agile Methodology?

Agile methodologies are based on the Agile Manifesto. They have increased focus on:

  1. Engagement with business users.
  2. Delivery of business value.

What is the Agile Manifesto?

In 2001, a group of software developers met to find common ground among their methodologies. This common ground became the Agile Manifesto. It states that they value:

Individuals and interactions Over processes and tools
Working software comprehensive documentation.
Customer collaboration contract negotiation.
Responding to change following a plan.

So, How will WorldVisitz benefit from Agile?

  • Agile uses iterations to deliver small partial increments of the whole application thus ensuring that at least part of the application is delivered early and that the customer starts seeing the system and receiving business value from it early.
  • Waterfall fixes requirements at the beginning of the project and assumes that there will be no change in the customer's requirements then proceeds in phases, creating the full application at one go.

Waterfall?

  • A waterfall methodology is sequential, phased process for software development where each phase produces a milestone that triggers the start of the next and a phase is not redone after it is completed.
  • Once requirements are established, they become a contract between the developer and the customer and it is almost impossible to change them after that.

Waterfall Vs Agile

  • Waterfall works well when both the customer's requirements and technology for implementation are well known.
  • If the requirements are not well understood or the technology is not well known, then Agile works well.
  • If both the requirements and the technology are unknown, then the project is in chaos and management has to intervene to ensure that at least one of the 2 is well understood then Agile can be used from that point.
